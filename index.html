Stroke Rehabilitation

  • Neuro-developmental treatment techniques
  • Bed mobility and transfer training
  • Caregiver training for daily support

Sports Injury Rehabilitation

Recovery programs for sprains, strains and ligament injuries, designed to safely return athletes and active individuals to full performance.

  • Injury-specific manual therapy
  • Strength and conditioning exercises
  • Return-to-sport progression plan
  • Injury-prevention guidance

Post-Surgery Rehabilitation

Safe, progressive physiotherapy after orthopedic or general surgery — including knee replacement, hip replacement and spine surgery — delivered at home.

  • Wound-safe early mobilization
  • Progressive strength rebuilding
  • Scar and swelling management
  • Return-to-routine activity plan

Back Pain Treatment

Evidence-based physiotherapy for acute and chronic back pain, combining manual therapy, posture correction and core strengthening.

  • Spinal mobilization techniques
  • Core and postural stabilization
  • Ergonomic and lifestyle advice
  • Long-term recurrence prevention

Neck Pain Treatment

Targeted treatment for neck stiffness and pain from poor posture, prolonged screen time, whiplash or cervical spondylosis.

  • Cervical mobilization and stretching
  • Posture retraining
  • Pain-relief therapeutic exercise
  • Workstation ergonomics advice

Knee Pain Physiotherapy

Physiotherapy for knee arthritis, ligament injuries and post-surgical knees to reduce pain and restore confident movement.

  • Quadriceps and hamstring strengthening
  • Joint mobilization techniques
  • Gait correction training
  • Pain and swelling management

Frozen Shoulder Treatment

Structured manual therapy and graded exercise to reduce stiffness and gradually restore full shoulder range of motion.

  • Capsular stretching techniques
  • Progressive range-of-motion exercises
  • Pain modulation therapy
  • Home exercise routine

Elderly Care Physiotherapy

Gentle, safety-focused physiotherapy for senior citizens to maintain strength, balance and independence at home.

  • Fall-prevention and balance training
  • Joint care and gentle mobility work
  • Mobility aid guidance
  • Bedridden patient care

Pain Management Physiotherapy

Drug-free, non-invasive techniques to manage chronic pain conditions and improve day-to-day quality of life.

  • Manual therapy and therapeutic exercise
  • Electrotherapy modalities (TENS)
  • Pain education and self-management
  • Long-term lifestyle adjustment plan
